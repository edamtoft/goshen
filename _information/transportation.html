Goshen Bus

All tickets are round trip and must be reserved.

Units are expected to provide at least one adult to ride on the bus with their campers. (Provisional campers are the exception.)

Units purchase seats and rosters are not necessary.

Going to Camp

Saturday pick-up: Bowman (All Weeks) & Marriott (units & provisional campers - Weeks 2, 3, 4, & 5)

Sunday pick-up: PMI (All Weeks), Ross (All Weeks), Olmsted (All Weeks), Lenhok'sin (All Weeks), & Marriott (units & provisional campers - Week 6)

Please arrive between 9:15am-9:45am to drop-off campers and check-in, unless otherwise notified.

Buses depart after 10am and arrive in Goshen after 1pm.

Returning from Camp

Saturday drop-off: All camps, all weeks

Please arrive by 10am to pick-up campers, unless otherwise notified.

Buses depart Goshen by 7am and arrive between 9:45am-10:15am.
