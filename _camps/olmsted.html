Meet Your
2016 Camp Director

Lauren Brown

This will be Lauren's 9th summer at Olmsted, and she has previously served as an Ecology Instructor, Ecology Director, Trailshead Director, and Program Director. She is known for always having a song on hand to sing, and her endless enthusiasm throughout the summer. She is incredibly excited for the summer to get started, and see everyones smiling faces when they arrive at Beautiful Camp Olmsted!
